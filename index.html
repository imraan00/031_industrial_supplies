ABOUT 031 INDUSTRIAL SUPPLIES

Established in 2016, 031 Industrial Supplies is a proudly Durban-based business supplying a comprehensive range of industrial products to businesses and individuals across KwaZulu-Natal. From safety wear and PPE to power tools, welding consumables, abrasives, fasteners, paint, chemicals, valves, brushware and steel fabrication — we are your one-stop industrial supply shop. Our name is a nod to our roots — the 031 Durban area code — and our commitment to serving the local industrial community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make workplace safety accessible and affordable for every business — from small contractors to large industrial operations. We believe that every worker deserves proper protection, and we work hard to ensure our products meet the highest safety standards at prices that won't break the budget.

Our Vision

To be the leading safety wear supplier in KwaZulu-Natal, known for our product quality, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service. We aim to grow alongside our clients by consistently expanding our product range and improving our delivery capabilities.

What We Offer

  • Safety Wear & PPE (SABS Certified)
  • Consumables & Welding Consumables
  • Abrasives (Grinding Discs, Sandpaper)
  • Fasteners (Bolts, Nuts, Screws & Anchors)
  • Hand Tools & Power Tools
  • Industrial Paint & Protective Coatings
  • Valves (Gate, Ball & Industrial)
  • Chemicals & Industrial Cleaning Agents
  • Brushware (Wire, Paint Brushes & Rollers)
  • Steel Fabrication Supplies

Delivery Service

We offer convenient door-to-door delivery in and around Durban, so you can get the safety gear you need without leaving the job site. Whether you're based in the CBD, surrounding industrial areas, or the greater eThekwini region, we've got you covered.
